-
- Organize and connect ideas
- Short **atomic** notes
- [[Fleeting notes]] (Thoughts, ideas, observations and anything that comes to your mind)
	- Fleeting notes lack structure and you just jot them down and throw them in your inbox
	- You are supposed to process them later and create [[Permanent notes]] After that you can delete them.
- [[Media Notes]] Concise and describes the main ideas of a media, like a book, video or article. It is critically important that you write your notes in your own words. This to make sure that you have understood.
- [[Permanent notes]] The Permanent notes are developed ideas and you use links to connect notes to each other. Should be clear, concise and only whole sentences. This represents your developing 2nd Brain. You should be able to understand a permanent note without having its context.
- Question: Is it unwise to create links between Media Notes?
